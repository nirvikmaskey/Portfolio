My artistic practice is about deep engagement with the stories and practices of places, people, objects, nature, and society. In todays fast-paced, extraction-driven world, I focus on the ecological, social, and cultural layers that are often erased or invisible. My process is rooted in experimental collaborations, involving activities such as listening, walking, drawing, talking, and playing. This leads me to work across diverse mediumsincluding painting, print, installations, film, audio, animation, and digital formatsdepending on the context and subject.
